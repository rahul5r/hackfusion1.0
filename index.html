About HackFusion 1.0

India's Most Innovative 24-Hour Hackathon Experience

Department of CSE - Data Science

Maharaja Institute of Technology Mysore

"Analyze, Visualize, Integrate"

HackFusion 1.0 is a prestigious 24-hour national-level hackathon organized by the Department of CSE - Data Science at Maharaja Institute of Technology Mysore. This event brings together the brightest minds from across the nation to innovate, collaborate, and create groundbreaking solutions.

Our hackathon focuses on four cutting-edge domains that are shaping the future of technology: Generative AI, Machine Learning, FinTech, and HealthTech. Participants will have the opportunity to work on real-world problems and showcase their creativity and technical expertise.

Generative AI

Generative AI is a cutting-edge technology that can generate text, images, code, music, and more. It learns from vast amounts of data to create original and human-like content..

üß†

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a branch of AI where systems learn patterns from data instead of being explicitly programmed. It powers applications like recommendation engines, fraud detection, and predictive analytics.

üí≥

FinTech

FinTech (Financial Technology) uses technology to innovate and improve financial services. It powers solutions like digital payments, lending, personal finance, blockchain, and fraud prevention.

HealthTech

HealthTech leverages technology to improve healthcare delivery, access, and outcomes. It includes innovations like telemedicine, wearable health devices, AI diagnostics, and digital health records.
